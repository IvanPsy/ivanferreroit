Zone d'Ombra Critiche (Non-Detto Strutturale)

Tutti gli studi lumping "SM use" (60 min watching body-positive TikTok = 60 min watching upward-comparison Instagram reels). Varianza clinicamente rilevante nascosta in "time spent." Soluzione: content analysis granulare, ma computationally/ethically difficult senza platform cooperation.
